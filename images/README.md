# Images Directory

Place your images in the appropriate folders:

## Attractions (`images/attractions/`)
Add images with these exact filenames:
- `ajinkyatara-fort.jpg` - Ajinkyatara Fort
- `thoseghar-waterfall.jpg` - Thoseghar Waterfall
- `kaas-plateau.jpg` - Kaas Plateau
- `sajjangad-fort.jpg` - Sajjangad Fort
- `koyna-dam.jpg` - Koyna Dam
- `chalkewadi-windmill.jpg` - Chalkewadi Windmill Farms

## Food (`images/food/`)
Add images with these exact filenames:
- `misal-pav.jpg` - Misal Pav dish
- `kandi-pedha.jpg` - Kandi Pedha sweet
- `zunka-bhakri.jpg` - Zunka Bhakri

## Image Guidelines
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended size**: 800x600px or similar aspect ratio (4:3)
- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High quality but web-optimized

## Where to Find Images
You can source images from:
- Your own photography
- Free stock photo sites (Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay)
- Local tourism websites (with proper attribution)
- Creative Commons licensed images
